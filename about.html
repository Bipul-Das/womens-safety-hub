About SafetyHub

Bridging the gap between emergency response and legal awareness.

The Motivation

In a world where safety remains a primary concern for women, access to immediate help and legal knowledge is often fragmented. While emergency numbers exist, they are not always at our fingertips. While laws exist, they are often buried in complex legal jargon.

SafetyHub was born from a simple question: "Where can a woman go to find help, understand her rights, and alert her loved ones, all in under 30 seconds?"

💡

Our Mission & Importance

This project aims to empower women through technology. It is not just an app; it is a digital companion designed to provide:

  • Immediate Action: One-click SOS alerts and direct emergency dialing.
  • Legal Literacy: Simplifying complex laws (Zero FIR, POSH) into plain language.
  • Digital Privacy: Ensuring user data is secure with features like "Quick Exit" and "Incognito Mode".
🎯
